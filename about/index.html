About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate in physics at Virginia Tech in the United States and I will defend for my PhD degree in the Spring of 2021. Rightnow I am on the job market for a postdoc position on theoretical high energy physics. My main research interests are supersymmetric quantum field theories, mirror symmetry, string theory as well as related mathematical topics.

Before my PhD study at Virginia Tech, I earned a master degree in theoretical physics at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in China.
