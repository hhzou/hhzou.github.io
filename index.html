I am currently a joint postdoc at YMSC, Tsinghua and BIMSA. I obtained my PhD in physics at Virginia Tech.

Research interests

My main research interests are supersymmetric quantum field theories, mirror symmetry, string theory as well as related mathematical topics. I am also interested in machine learning in theoretical high energy physics.
